About CopyrightShield

Born from a deep understanding of Trust & Safety operations, CopyrightShield was founded to solve a problem we know all too well: the manual, time-consuming, and legally complex process of brand protection.

Our mission is to empower creators and businesses with a powerful, automated tool that is backed by real-world operational expertise. We believe that protecting your intellectual property shouldn't be a full-time job.

  • Built by T&S Professionals: Our team understands the nuance of takedowns.
