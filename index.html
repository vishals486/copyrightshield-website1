Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your service different from others in the market?

Many takedown services are built on older technologies that struggle with new-age platforms. We are a modern, agile startup built specifically for the web3 era. Our AI agents are designed to be adaptive and find hidden infringements that legacy systems often miss, giving you a faster and more effective takedown process.

How do your AI agents find infringement on new platforms?

Our agents are designed to be adaptive. They learn from new types of piracy, using a combination of public APIs, advanced web scraping, and content recognition to find content on platforms like Gofile and Wetransfer, as well as decentralized social media.

What happens if a takedown notice is ignored or rejected?

Our system automatically escalates the issue. We'll send notices to the hosting provider and can assist in getting the content removed from search engine results to minimize its visibility.
